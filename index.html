About

Powderfinger is a Neil Young tribute band from Minnesota featuring Mark “Hazzy” Hasbrouck (Lead vocals, Guitar, Harmonica), Jim Gruidl (Guitar, Vocals), Jim Bjorklun (Bass, Vocals) and Andy Deckard (Drums, Vocals).

For over 20 years, Powderfinger has been performing the #1 hits and deep album cuts that are favorites of Neil Young fans young and old. Going back to the early years of Buffalo Springfield and CSNY through to Crazy Horse and beyond.

They easily create the Neil Young and Crazy Horse sound everyone loves. Expect long jams on classics like “Down by the River”, “Cowgirl in the Sand” and “Rockin' in the Free World” with not-to-be-missed songs like “Old Man,” “Heart of Gold” and “Harvest Moon”. It's a fulfilling musical experience for any fan of Neil Young’s music.
